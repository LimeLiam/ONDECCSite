The ONDECC project film studio is an association of people interested in film production or related arts working together to produce a variety of works. We take a unique approach in our production technique to maximize artistic freedom and to ensure that everyone can try their hand at different aspects of the production based on their varied interests.

Film production is a unification of the arts in all areas from theatre to creative writing which is why we are always looking for people with experience/interests in any of the following:
-Film
-Theatre (Film cast)
-Creative writing (plot/screen writing)
-Music production (soundtrack and audio production)
