What We Do

The ONDECC Project is an innovative nonprofit organization that aims to promote professional career exploration, foster creative platforms for the arts, and implement impactful social and community service initiatives. We aim to build nationwide communities of people who share similar interests and values to fulfill our mission of fostering professional and artistic exploration and achievement.

Programs

We believe that everyone deserves a shot and that everyone has a passion- something that they are amazing at! We celebrate intellectual diversity and will work hard to ensure that every person realizes their potential and finds their passion. We hope that you join us in finding new passions, possibilities, and hopefully even a future career!

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower emerging innovators and artists by
- Fostering curiosity and exploration of a multitude of career focuses
- Building a platform where people can freely create and explore unique ideas and interests and
-Engineering service and social initiatives to make a larger impact
