What is membership?The What

Members get full access to all available programs and experiences as well as exclusive benefits like access to special events and resources. Joining is quick, easy, and available to anyone. Join today to take advantage of the full range of unique opportunities.

If you are a registered member of your school club and have been added to the main discord server, you are already a member, no further application required!

BenefitsThe Why

As a member of The ONDECC Project, you will:

-Have access to our full range of programs and opportunities
-Be invited to exclusive events
-Have opportunities to lead meaningful service and/or design projects
-Join a professional network of people with similar interests to yours

How to applyThe How

To apply, first check to see if you’re school already has an ONDECC Project club. If your school does, enter through your school’s club and contact the club president for application information. If your school does not have a club, consider starting one. If neither is possible, you may enter individually.

If are unsure about your school’s status, contact your region’s club manager.

To enter as an individual, contact your region’s club manager with your name, where you’re from, and a message saying you would like to apply as an individual.

If you need any help or for more information, contact your school club president, your region’s club manager, or us!
